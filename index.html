Our Story

The Baker Behind
the Magic ✨

Tiers of Joy grew from a simple truth — that a truly special cake can make someone feel completely seen and celebrated. It started with family, then word spread (along with the frosting, honestly).

Everything is made from scratch. Real butter, fresh eggs, no box mixes, no shortcuts. Just honest ingredients and skilled hands that turn your ideas into something edible and unforgettable.

That Lexus car? Made for my son Aaron's birthday 🚗 — and yes, he cried (the good kind!). Every single order gets that same personal love and detail.
